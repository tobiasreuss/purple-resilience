About Purple Resilience

We're building climate risk intelligence tools for critical infrastructure operators who need actionable insights, not academic reports.

Purple Resilience was founded to address a growing challenge in critical infrastructure: the need for actionable, operationally relevant risk intelligence in the face of increasing weather volatility and climate-driven operational risk.

Grid operators face an increasingly complex operational environment. Extreme weather events are more frequent, vegetation management resources are constrained, and regulatory pressure for resilience planning is intensifying. Yet many existing tools provide climate projections or weather data without translating these signals into operational decisions.

We believe infrastructure operators deserve better. Our focus is on translating environmental signals into granular, asset-level risk intelligence that integrates directly into operational workflows—prioritizing inspections, guiding vegetation management, enabling proactive preparedness, and supporting investment decisions.

Our Approach

We combine expertise in atmospheric science, geospatial data analysis, machine learning, and energy infrastructure operations. Our models are informed by physics, calibrated with real-world data, and designed to produce outputs that operators can use immediately.

We're building Purple Resilience specifically for the European grid operator community, starting with design partnerships that help us refine our models and deliver intelligence that operators actually need.
