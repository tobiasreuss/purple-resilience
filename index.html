Applications

Use cases for grid resilience

01

Vegetation Management Prioritization

Identify high-risk line segments where vegetation growth, species type, and weather exposure combine to create elevated wildfire or outage risk. Focus inspection and trimming resources where they matter most.

02

Wildfire Preparedness Planning

Anticipate wildfire risk windows with weather-driven early warning signals. Adjust crew readiness, pre-position equipment, and communicate with emergency services based on regional risk forecasts.

03

Asset Risk Screening

Assess exposure of substations, transmission lines, and distribution feeders to weather-related stressors. Support investment planning and protection system design with risk-informed data.

04

Post-Event Impact Triage

After storms or wildfire events, rapidly identify which network segments are most likely damaged or at risk. Accelerate damage assessment and restoration prioritization.

05

Internal Risk Reporting

Provide transparent, data-backed risk assessments to internal risk management, compliance, and executive teams. Support regulatory reporting and stakeholder communication.
